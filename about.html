HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Al Arabiya Finance was founded in 2016 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. With several years of experience in the finance industry, our team recognised a large gap in the development sector that needed to be filled, however, finding the right product to fill this gap was the challenge.

In 2019 Al Arabiya developed its own unique Equity Participation Funding Program and is proud to offer it to accredited developers and entrepreneurs, making it easier to transform dreams into reality.

With the success of this exclusive product we were able to expand WorldWide, opening an office in Dubai, United Arab Emirate in 2019.
