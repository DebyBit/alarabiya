Al Arabiya Finance Small Cap Program

Investors looking for better returns are starting to move away from standard investments like shares, stocks and bonds and moving their funds to Small Cap Programs (SCP).

 

Private Placement Programs traditionally have been the domain of institutional investors or high net worth individuals. However, since 2018 and the introduction of Fully Managed 100% Capital Protected Private Placement Programs it is now easier than ever for traditional investors to participate in Small Caps Programs that deliver high returns.

 

Because of the nature of entering into Private Placement Programs, Al Arabiya has taken the work out of clients wanting to enter these programs, because we want to make it easy for our clients to get the kind of investment returns that were only available to investors who had $100 million or more. In that regard Al Arabiya has done the work by going through the process and has cleared compliance and in doing so we deal directly with the “platform” which is the trading group or “trader”.

 

With prudent Risk Management, our Small Caps Program has consistently achieved outstanding high results and are a safe and secure investment.

The process is similar to a full trading program except:
• The financial entry level is only USD/Euro 1 million
• The Client is an Al Arabiya investor
• Our clients funds will be added to our existing program which compounds monthly.
• We can also work with acceptable bank instruments. This process takes a little longer.
• There will be a Private Placement Agreement, which will include an estimated payout over
12 months, which is the term of the program.
There are No upfront fees
There are also rollover and compound features available.
